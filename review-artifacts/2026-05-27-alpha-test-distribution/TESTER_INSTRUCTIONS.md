# Tester Instructions

*These instructions are embedded inside the AlphaTesterGate component as the consent screen. This document is the source of truth for what testers see and are asked to do.*

---

## What you're looking at

This is a prototype coordination environment — it simulates an event day in progress. Things are moving. Vendors are arriving. The run of show is live.

Your job is simple: **explore it like you're on shift.** Tap things. Scroll. React. You don't need to understand everything — that's the point.

## Screen recording — do this first

Before you tap Begin, start a screen recording on your phone.

**iPhone:**
1. Swipe down from the top-right corner to open Control Center
2. Tap the Screen Recording button (circle inside a circle)
3. A 3-second countdown appears — then recording starts
4. Tap Begin in this app

**Android:**
1. Swipe down from the top to open Quick Settings
2. Find "Screen recorder" and tap it
3. Choose "Record audio" if prompted → tap Start
4. Return to this app and tap Begin

*If you can't find screen recording, a screen recording is helpful but not required. The interaction data the app collects on its own is sufficient.*

## During the session

- Explore freely — there's no wrong thing to tap
- Say what you're thinking out loud if you're comfortable (recording will capture it)
- If something confuses you, stay with it for a moment before moving on
- If something feels wrong or broken, note it — that's exactly what I need to know
- Session ends when you feel done, or after 10 minutes

## Ending the session

When you're ready to finish:
1. Stop your screen recording (tap the red bar at the top → Stop)
2. Tap the **End Session** button at the bottom of the screen
3. Answer the 9 questions that follow — be direct, not generous
4. On the final screen, tap **Copy Export** to copy your session data
5. Paste the copied text into an email and send to todd@toddwillisphoto.com
6. Attach your screen recording to the same email

## What the app collects

- Which elements you tapped, and when
- How long you paused before tapping
- How far you scrolled
- Whether you left and returned to the app
- Your survey responses

All data stays on your device until you copy and send the export. Nothing is transmitted automatically.
